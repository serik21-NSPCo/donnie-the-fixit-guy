Rekey services

Most of the time, your locks don’t actually need to be replaced. If you just bought a home, had keys go missing, or want to make sure old keys no longer work, rekeying is usually the best value.

I can rekey your locks so you no longer have to worry about old copies of keys floating around, and I can often simplify things so one key works across multiple doors. If replacement or upgraded hardware makes more sense, I can help with that too.

Security concerns & lock upgrades

Do you have a door that never seems to latch quite right? A lock that feels loose, sloppy, or one hard turn away from failing? Maybe you’re just not confident your current setup is actually protecting your home or office.

I can assess your doors, locks, and hardware, point out weak spots, and recommend practical upgrades — from small improvements to bigger security changes.

Accessibility solutions that make life easier

One of the most overlooked parts of locksmithing is how much it can improve accessibility. Sometimes that means simple changes like easier-to-grip knobs, levers, or custom keys. Other times it means creating a seamless entry experience for someone with mobility challenges or special needs.

I can install smart locks, automate locking, and help create easier entry solutions with things like remote access, automatic locking, and assisted door operation. The goal is simple: make your home easier to live in, without sacrificing safety or security.
