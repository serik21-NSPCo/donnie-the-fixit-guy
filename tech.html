Meet Donnie

Your local Fix-It Guy

I’ve been a locksmith since 2004, starting out in a small shop learning the trade from my dad. Over the years, I’ve worked everywhere from casinos to commercial door installs to advanced access control systems.

These days, I specialize in solving the kinds of problems that don’t have simple answers — combining hands-on repair work with smart home and technology solutions.

The “Send Donnie” Guy

At every company I’ve worked for, I’ve been the guy they call when something gets tricky. The jobs where the wiring is a mess, systems don’t talk to each other, or nobody quite knows the right answer.

Instead of contractors pointing fingers, you get one person who can handle it end to end.

Design it. Install it. Wire it. Fix it. Explain it.

Hands-On + Tech-Savvy

Over the years, my work naturally evolved into the tech side of things. I’ve designed and installed access control systems, integrated power door operators, built custom software solutions, and managed IT infrastructure.

The lock and security industry has become increasingly tech-heavy — and that’s where I thrive.

Why I Do This

I’ve lived in the Hastings area for over 10 years and grew up nearby in Cottage Grove. You might know me as “Donnie the Bingo Guy” from Boombox Bingo around town.

I started this business to have the flexibility to be there for my family — especially my youngest son, who has special needs and keeps me on my toes.

This work lets me take care of both my customers and my family — and that balance means everything.

How I Work

I keep things simple. I’ll give you an honest recommendation, explain your options clearly, and provide a fair quote.

No pressure. No upselling. No nonsense.

If I do the work, I stand behind it — and I’m always happy to help with ongoing support for smart home systems, cameras, or networks.

I’m friendly, respectful, and I treat every home like it’s my own.
